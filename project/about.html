About Retronomus

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Retronomus began as a passion project between a group of vintage technology enthusiasts. What started as a small online marketplace for trading retro computers has grown into a comprehensive platform for preserving computing history.

We believe these devices tell the story of technological progress and deserve to be remembered, restored, and in some cases, still used today.

Our Mission

Retronomus exists to:

  • Preserve important pieces of computing history
  • Connect collectors with rare and hard-to-find items
  • Provide resources for restoring vintage technology
  • Educate new generations about tech history
  • Build a community of retro tech enthusiasts
