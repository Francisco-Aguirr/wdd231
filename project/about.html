About Retronomus

Legal

Retronomus is an academic e-commerce project created solely for educational purposes. This website serves as a practical application of web development principles learned in class,

including front-end design, responsive layouts, JavaScript functionality, and local storage management for cart operations.

Key Features

Retronomus exists to:

  • Fictional Product Showcase: All products displayed on Retronomus are simulated and do not represent real inventory.
  • Brand Disclaimer: Any logos, product names, or trademarks mentioned are the property of their respective owners and are used here purely for demonstration purposes.
  • Interactive Elements: The site includes a functional shopping cart, product filtering, modals, and responsive design techniques.
  • Learning-Focused: Built to practice HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and DOM manipulation without commercial intent.
