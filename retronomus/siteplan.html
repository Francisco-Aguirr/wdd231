Site Name

Retronomus

This name combines "retro" (representing vintage/classic elements) and "nomus" (suggesting organization or management). It reflects our focus on cataloging, selling, and celebrating retro technology, games, and culture.

Domain suggestion: retronomus.com (includes e-commerce functionality)

Site Purpose

The Retronomus website will serve as both a comprehensive resource and marketplace for retro technology enthusiasts, providing:

  • An e-commerce platform for buying/selling vintage tech and games
  • Shopping cart and secure checkout system
  • User accounts with purchase history
  • A catalog of vintage technology and games with pricing information
  • Historical information about retro computing
  • Community forums for discussion
  • Restoration guides and tutorials
  • Wishlist and collection tracking features

E-Commerce Features

  • Product listings with high-quality images and detailed descriptions
  • Condition grading system (Mint, Good, Fair, For Parts)
  • Secure payment processing
  • Shipping cost calculator
  • Customer reviews and ratings
  • Return policy and buyer protection

Scenarios

User scenarios highlighting the shopping cart functionality:

Scenario 1: The Collector Making a Purchase

"I found several vintage game cartridges I want to buy. How can I add them to my cart, see the total with shipping, and complete my purchase securely?"

Scenario 2: The Seller Managing Inventory

"I have some retro computer parts to sell. How do I list them on Retronomus, manage my inventory, and process orders when they sell?"

Scenario 3: The Casual Browser

"I'm not ready to buy yet but I see some items I might want later. Can I save them to a wishlist or get notified if they go on sale?"

E-Commerce Specific Usage:

  • Add to Cart Buttons: Secondary color (#E74C3C) for high visibility
  • Checkout Progress: Primary color (#2A3F54) for completed steps, Accent (#F39C12) for current step
  • Price Tags/Sale Badges: Secondary color with white text
  • Cart Icon/Counter: Accent color with white number
  • Form Validation: Secondary color for errors, a green success color (not in palette) for success

Typography

Font selections optimized for e-commerce readability:

Montserrat (Heading Font)

Used for product titles, section headers, and call-to-action buttons.

Roboto (Body Font)

Used for product descriptions, pricing information, and all body text. Provides excellent readability for longer product details.

E-Commerce Typography Considerations

  • Prices: Montserrat bold with secondary color for emphasis
  • Product Cards: Consistent typography hierarchy (Title > Price > Description)
  • Checkout Forms: Roboto for optimal form readability
